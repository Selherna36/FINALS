Opened in 2009...

A small hole-in-the-wall located on Shields and Ashlan opened its doors to wing lovers in Fresno! The Native American themed resturuant is quite literally a small stack of bricks that holds the secret to THE BEST WINGS in the Central Valley. With up to 10 different sauces ranging from sweet, savory, and "ATOMIC", there are choices for everyone! Not to mention the affordable prices, unlike SOME competitors. Buffalo Ranch is the place to go if you want great service, and even better wings!
